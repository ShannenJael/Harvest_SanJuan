Elevate Student Ministry

Helping students know Jesus better and to make Him known

Elevate Student Ministry serves 7th-12th grade students with the goal to help teens know Jesus better and to make Him known. We believe the teenage years are critical for establishing a firm foundation of faith.

Evangelism

Sharing the gospel with lost students and equipping teens to be witnesses in their schools and communities.

Discipleship

Teaching biblical truths that help students navigate the challenges of their teen years with wisdom and faith.

Fellowship

Building spiritually encouraging friendships and creating a community where students feel they belong.

Worship

Fostering both private and corporate worship of God through music, prayer, and devotion.

Ministry

Encouraging student participation in Christian service and discovering their spiritual gifts.
