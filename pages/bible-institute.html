Faith Bible Institute

Equipping Believers for Life and Ministry

Every Word, Every Church, Every Christian

Faith Bible Institute (FBI) is a three-year Bible college program designed to be offered through local churches. At Harvest Baptist Church San Juan, we are proud to host this comprehensive biblical education program that equips believers for life and ministry.

Whether you're a new believer wanting to grow in your faith or a seasoned Christian seeking deeper study of God's Word, FBI offers systematic training through video instruction and group discussion.

Program Overview

Three-Year Program

A comprehensive curriculum covering the entire Bible, including Old Testament surveys, New Testament epistles, Hebrew wisdom literature, and much more.

Semester Schedule

Classes are organized by semesters. Spring semester typically begins mid-January, with fall semesters starting in mid-August.

Group Learning

Study alongside fellow believers in a supportive church environment with video-based instruction and group discussion.

Online Option

Can't attend in person? Online course options are available for students who need flexibility in their schedule.

Sample Courses

Here's a glimpse of what you'll study in the FBI program

The Judges & the Single Kingdom of Israel

Survey the period of the judges and the united kingdom under Saul, David, and Solomon.

New Testament Epistles

Study James, Galatians, 1-2 Thessalonians, Romans, and other letters to the early church.

Hebrew Wisdom Literature

Explore Psalms, Proverbs, Ecclesiastes, and the Song of Solomon.

Tuition & Fees

  • First Semester: $157 per student
  • Subsequent Semesters: $115 per student
  • Online Students: Additional $60 online fee per semester

Early Bird Discount: Enroll before July 1st for special savings!
