Children's Ministry

"Train Up A Child in the Way that He Should Go" - Proverbs 22:6

Our Children's Ministry is dedicated to teaching children the truths of God's Word through age-appropriate programs and activities in a safe, fun, and nurturing environment.

Sunday School

When: Sundays at 9:30 AM

Preschool (Ages 3-5)

Simple Bible stories and songs introduce young hearts to Jesus.

Primaries (Grades 1-2)

Learning activities and basic lessons build a foundation of faith.

Juniors (Grades 3-6)

In-depth Bible truths and character development prepare kids for life.

Nursery

Ages: 0-3 years

Available: During all services

Our nursery provides a safe, protected, clean, and enjoyable environment for your little ones. Our qualified workers care for your children with love while you worship. Parents receive a pager so you can be reached if needed.

Children's Church

When: Sunday Morning Service

Children's Church features enthusiastic singing, games, and Bible stories presented weekly in a way kids can understand and enjoy. It's church designed just for them!
