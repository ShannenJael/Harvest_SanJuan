Our Ministries

Whether you are a child or a Senior Saint, Harvest has a ministry for you

MINISTRIES

Soul Winning

Soul winning is the personal work of sharing the Gospel and leading individuals to a saving knowledge of the Lord Jesus Christ. Through prayer, personal conversations, and follow-up, we seek to guide people toward salvation and help new believers grow in their faith.

Door Knocking

Door knocking is a house-to-house ministry where church members visit homes within the community to share the Gospel and extend a warm invitation to attend church services. This ministry allows us to personally reach families, pray for their needs, and introduce them to the love of Christ.

Outreaches

Outreaches are organized evangelistic activities that bring the message of Christ beyond the church walls, especially to children and their families. Through Bible lessons, games, and fellowship, we aim to share the Gospel and plant seeds of faith in the hearts of those we reach.

Bible School

Bible School is a ministry dedicated to teaching the doctrines of the Bible and guiding believers in the practical application of God's Word in their daily lives. Through systematic lessons and faithful instruction from Scripture, students gain a deeper understanding of biblical truths, strengthening their faith and equipping them to live a Christ-centered life and serve the Lord more effectively.

Tract Distribution

This ministry focuses on sharing the Gospel through printed tracts in public places, neighborhoods, and community gatherings. These materials clearly present the message of salvation and serve as a witness that individuals can read and reflect on even after the encounter.

Bible Study

Bible Study provides a time of learning and deeper understanding of God's Word. Through careful teaching and discussion, believers are encouraged to grow spiritually and apply biblical truths in their daily lives.

Missions

Missions is the ministry of bringing the Gospel to other communities, cities, and nations. By supporting missionaries and church-planting efforts, we take part in fulfilling the Great Commission to reach the world for Christ.

Visitation

Visitation involves reaching out to church members, new converts, and those who may have been absent from services. Through personal visits, we strengthen fellowship, offer encouragement, and provide spiritual care.

Choir

The choir leads the congregation in worship through special music that honors and glorifies the Lord. Through songs of praise, the choir helps prepare hearts for worship and the preaching of God's Word.

Music Ministry

The music ministry supports the worship services through singing, instruments, and sound coordination. Its purpose is to uplift the congregation and communicate biblical truths through Christ-honoring music.

Teachers' Training

Teachers' Training equips Sunday School teachers and ministry workers with biblical understanding and effective teaching methods. This ministry helps ensure that God's Word is taught clearly and faithfully to learners of all ages.

Sports Ministry

Sports Ministry uses athletic activities as a way to build friendships and reach people with the Gospel. This mostly focuses on the Men's fellowship. Through teamwork, this ministry creates opportunities to share biblical values and encourage spiritual growth.

Youth Ministry

The Youth Ministry is dedicated to guiding young people to know Christ, grow in their faith, and live for Him. Through Bible teaching, fellowship, and activities, the youth are encouraged to serve the Lord in the days of their youth.

Social Media Ministry

The Social Media Ministry uses online platforms to share the Gospel, promote church activities, and encourage believers through biblical content. It extends the church's outreach by reaching people beyond physical gatherings.

Police Officers Bible Study

This ministry conducts Bible studies specifically for police officers, providing spiritual guidance and encouragement from God's Word. It seeks to strengthen them in their service while pointing them to Christ as the source of wisdom, strength, and salvation.
