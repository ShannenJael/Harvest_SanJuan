Adult Ministry

Come to Christ in worship, connect with the church, grow deeper in the Lord with others, serve in the ways God has gifted you, and go spread the Good News.

Visitation & Outreach

Members pair up weekly to witness in the community. Monthly Gospel Blitz events distribute gospel materials in neighborhood packets.

Music Ministry

Member choir performing uplifting music with various ensembles and soloists. Cantatas presented throughout the year.

SUPER Saints

Active fellowship group for seniors meeting monthly with meals, programs, fellowship, and inspirational messages.

Discipled Veteran Ministry

Monthly meetings (first Wednesday, 7:00 PM) designed to help veterans adjust post-service through peer support and spiritual guidance.

Faith Bible Institute

Online Bible study classes covering chronological and doctrinal study with interactive DVD formats and workbooks.

Social Fellowships

International Missions Banquets, Mother/Daughter Banquets, church picnics, and retreats at Camp Victory.

Missions

"Go ye therefore, and teach all nations..." - Matthew 28:19

Harvest Baptist Church San Juan identifies as ambassadors for Christ committed to the Great Commission. We believe in proclaiming the Gospel globally across all nations, tribes, and language groups.

Faith Promise Missions

Our annual Faith Promise Missions initiative allows members to contribute funds beyond their regular tithe to support international missionaries and ministries. This program launches each year following our Missions Conference, where missionaries from around the world present their ministry needs and vision.
