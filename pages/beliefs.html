What We Believe

Our Statement of Faith

At Harvest Baptist Church San Juan, we hold firmly to the fundamental truths of the Christian faith as revealed in the Holy Scriptures. The following statements represent our core doctrinal beliefs.

The Holy Scriptures

We believe that the Holy Scriptures of the Old and New Testament are verbally and plenary inspired and serve as the final authority for faith and practice. The King James Version is designated as our official translation.

The Godhead

We believe that one eternal God exists in three persons - Father, Son, and Holy Spirit - each coequal in nature, power, and glory with identical attributes.

The Person and Work of Christ

We believe that Jesus Christ is the eternal Son of God who became human without ceasing to be divine. His death on the cross provides vicarious, substitutionary atonement, with justification secured through His literal physical resurrection.

The Person of Christ

We believe in the Deity, virgin birth, and bodily resurrection of Jesus Christ.

The Person and Work of the Holy Spirit

We believe that the Holy Spirit convicts of sin, effects regeneration, indwells believers, and distributes spiritual gifts. Sign gifts like speaking in tongues were temporary and are not necessary evidence of Spirit-filling.

All Men Are Sinful and Lost

All men are born sinners by nature and by actions and are condemned already, needing redemption.

Salvation by Grace Through Faith

We believe that salvation is received only by personal faith in the Lord Jesus Christ as a free gift, not earned through human merit or works. "For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of God." (Ephesians 2:8)

The Eternal Security and Assurance of Believers

We believe that believers, once saved, are kept by God's power and are thus secure in Christ forever. Nothing can separate the true believer from the love of God.

The Church

We believe that the true church comprises only regenerated believers and operates with local church autonomy, recognizing baptism by immersion and the Lord's Supper as ordinances.

Separation

Christians are to remain unspotted from the world, holy, separate from sinful living and worldly corruption.

The Second Coming of Christ

We believe Christ will return for his church and we will meet him in the air before the Great Tribulation.

The Return of Christ

We believe in the visible, personal, and pre-millennial return of Jesus Christ.

The Eternal State

The Lord Jesus will rule and reign forever in the New Heavens and New Earth. The believers will rule with him, unbelievers will be cast into eternal torment in the Lake of Fire.

Creation

We believe the universe was created by God in six literal 24-hour days as recorded in Genesis. We reject evolution and related theories as unscriptural.

Have Questions?

We'd love to discuss these beliefs with you and answer any questions you may have.
